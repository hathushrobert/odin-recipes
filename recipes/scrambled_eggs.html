Scrambled eggs

Description

Okay hear me out; Lots of people actually don't know how to make this simple yet delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Oil
  • milk

Steps

  1. Add oil to a frying pan
  2. Crack the eggs and pour them into the pan
  3. Use the spatula to break the yolk membrane. Let the yolk mix evenly
  4. Add milk
  5. Turn the flame up and add salt and pepper
  6. Keep mixing around with the spatula
  7. Once the egg starts to become solid, turn the stove off
  8. Make sure the eggs are scrambled to a size of your liking
  9. Enjoy your eggs with Toast :D
