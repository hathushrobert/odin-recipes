Pizza

Description

Pizza has Italian origins and is very popular world wide!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Yeast
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olives
  • Tomato sauce
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water and olive oil and make the dough
  2. Add yeast and let it rise
  3. Knead the dough until its just right (idk)
  4. Flatten the dough into a round pizza shape
  5. Smear tomato sauce and grate cheese onto it
  6. Sprinkle chicken pieces
  7. Add the pizza into the oven and wait for 15 minutes
  8. Take the pizza out and enjoy your meal :D
