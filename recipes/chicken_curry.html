Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken Curry is very popular in Asia and also other parts of the world. This recipe will follow a Sri Lankan style of cooking!

Ingredients

  • Chicken (Obviously)
  • Salt, Pepper, Chilli Powder
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Curry leaves
  • Tomatos
  • Coconut milk

Steps

  1. Wash the vegetables and chop them up
  2. Defrost the chicken and clean and wash it
  3. Add salt, peper and chilli powder to a bowl with the chicken in it
  4. Mix well and leave it to be seasoned
  5. Light the stove, add oil and garlic and onions. Fry until they are golden brown
  6. Add the chicken and let it cook. Add more water if needed
  7. After about 20 minutes, add the tomatoes and the coconut milk
  8. Stir it, add more salt or chilli if necessary
  9. Your curry is ready :D
